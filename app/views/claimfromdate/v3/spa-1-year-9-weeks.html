Your State Pension date

You reached State Pension age on 5 May 2021 and may be owed some payments.

How do you want to get any State Pension you are owed?
Up to 12 months of State Pension we owe you will be backdated and we'll pay it along with your first payment. You will not get any State Pension for the period 5 May 2021 to 11 July 2022.
Your State Pension could increase by just over 0.1% for every week that you have delayed since 5 May 2021. It takes over 17 years to get back the money you use to increase your payments.
You can split what you are owed between a one-off payment and an increase to your regular payments.
