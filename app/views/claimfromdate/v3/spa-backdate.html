Backdate your State Pension

You can backdate your State Pension for up to 12 months.

You will get a one-off payment to cover the period from the date you enter to 14 July 2022.

Your regular payments will also increase by just over 0.1% for every week between 5 June 2021 and the day before the one you enter.
