Delay your start date

To increase your regular payments, start your State Pension from 14 June 2022 or later.

Your State Pension could increase by just over 0.1% for every week that you delay, as long as you delay for at least 9 weeks.

It could take around 18 years to get back the money you delay taking and you will not get any payments before the date you enter.

Error messages:

Incomplete - Date must include a day, month and year
Invalid characters - Enter a real date, like 12 2 2019
Date before today - Date must be 25 7 2022 or in the future
